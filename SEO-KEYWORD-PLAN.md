Search volume and exact difficulty must be confirmed with Google Search Console and Google Ads Keyword Planner after deployment; the project deliberately does not invent keyword-volume numbers.

## The “best web development company in India” search

The site should support this search, but it should not repeatedly claim to be “the best” without independent proof. Searches containing “best” and “top” commonly show comparison articles and agency directories because users are trying to compare several providers.

MITOMS should pursue this query through both channels:

1. Build authority for the MITOMS web-development page through strong case studies, reviews, links and useful content.
2. Earn inclusion on reputable comparison and agency-review platforms through complete profiles, verified client reviews and editorial outreach.

Recommended profile and outreach categories:

- Verified agency-review platforms
- Indian and Delhi NCR technology directories
- Client and partner websites
- Founder and company profiles
- Industry publications and genuine comparison articles
- Google Business Profile for local visibility

## Search Console keyword groups

Create separate tracking groups for:

1. Brand: MITOMS, MITOMS Technologies
2. National commercial: web development company in India, custom software development company in India
3. Local: Ghaziabad and Delhi NCR searches
4. Service: mobile apps, UI/UX, cloud, AI and IT consulting
5. Informational: cost, comparison and selection questions
6. Case studies and industry searches

Measure clicks, impressions, click-through rate, average position, leads and revenue by landing page. Ranking alone is not the business goal.
